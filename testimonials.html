Testimonials

Personal stories and reflections from the participants.

Photo
"Cultural exchange, based on interacting with people from different countries and participating in debates, helped me better understand the differences between cultures and how they influence people’s thinking and behavior. I learned that people can become changemakers, but not everyone becomes a truly great one, which shows that involvement, perseverance, and a real desire to create change are essential."

– Andru, Romania

Photo
"This project is special to me because it was my first experience of this kind, and at the same time, I learned many things from the people I met here, while also hoping that I will have the opportunity to see them again in the future. The debate workshop was my favorite because it gave me the opportunity to hear different opinions and varied arguments, thus contributing to my personal and intellectual development."

– Anna M., Serbia

Photo
"Cooperation activities were very important to me because they helped me develop my teamwork skills and collaborate more effectively with the other participants. I learned some Italian gestures, which helped me better understand Italian culture and the importance of nonverbal communication in social interactions."

– Elif, Turkey

Photo
"I learned that volunteering can have a significant impact on both professional life and personal development, while also giving me a clearer perspective on the realities of rural areas and the importance of not giving up. I explored examples of local volunteering and the way people involved do not give up, managing to create meaningful changes in their communities, such as initiatives in the medical field, for example an oncology hospital."

– Arcangelo, Italy

Photo
"During this project, I learned how important effective communication is between people from different cultures, as it facilitates mutual understanding and helps build relationships based on respect and collaboration. The activity I enjoyed the most was teamwork because it gave me the opportunity to express my ideas, listen to others’ opinions, and work together to achieve common results."

– Iulian, Romania

Photo
"My favorite discussion topic was cultural diversity because it gave me the opportunity to discover different traditions, customs, and perspectives, contributing to a better understanding of the world around me. I learned that being a changemaker means not only having good ideas, but also taking concrete action, being persistent, and taking responsibility for creating positive change in your community."

– Eslem, Turkey
