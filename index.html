What was this project about?

Young people from rural and remote areas often face structural barriers to employability, including limited access to quality education, entrepreneurship support, professional networks, and digital opportunities.

These challenges disproportionately affect even more specifically NEET youth from rural areas, increasing the risk of social and economic exclusion and limiting their participation in the labour market and civic life.

At the same time Young people often want to contribute to their communities but feel that “nothing changes,” which fuels apathy and disengagement. Thus our exhcnage focuses on promoting volunteering and entrpeneurship as alternative ways for rural and NEET youth. This youth exchange increases engagement and enhance activization of rural and NEET young people by using volunteering and social entrepreneurship as hands-on tools to fight apathy and unemployment.

Participants discuss common challenges and realities, co-create possible solutions, discover together volunteering value and benefits, explore entrpreneurship as a possible way by building the right mindset.
